Refund Policy

Please review the refund and replacement terms before completing your purchase.

Refund Eligibility

If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect item, contact us within 48 hours of delivery with clear photos and your order details.

Approved Refunds

  • Approved refunds are initiated to the original payment method.
  • Bank and payment gateway processing may take 5 to 10 business days.
  • If a replacement is preferred and stock is available, we can arrange that instead of a refund.

Non-Returnable Cases

Items that are used, opened, or damaged after delivery may not qualify for refund unless there is a verified product issue.
